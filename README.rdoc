== Launcher Sleepy, All the tweets with #launcher_sleepy in one place. http://launchersleepy.heroku.com

LauncherSleepy allows Launch Academy students to post photos of their classmates who have fallen asleep from exhaustion!!!

Simply log on to your twitter account, post the photo and use #launcher_sleepy in order to have it post to the launcher sleepy website, http://launchersleepy.heroku.com .
